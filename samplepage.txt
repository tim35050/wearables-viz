          <h5>About Footlogger</h5>
          The 3L Labs Footlogger is a wearable fitness tracking device that aims at spotting health problems early, as well as logging daily activity. Placed in the user's shoe, 8 sensors coupled to 1 accelerometer help identify and record exercise habits. The data is then disclosed to the user via text or smartphone app. This computing device's technology can be used for athletes training, regular everyday workouts and rehabilitation.         			</div>
